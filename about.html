About CvSU Online School Store

Official Provider of Academic Essentials

Our Mission

The CvSU Online School Store aims to provide convenient access to school essentials for all Cavite State University students through a reliable and user-friendly online platform. We are committed to supporting student success by ensuring that necessary academic materials are always within reach.

Official Merchandise

We take pride in offering authentic CvSU apparel and supplies. Our products are designed to foster university pride and provide students with high-quality items that represent the excellence of our institution.

What We Provide

Books & Textbooks

Required reading materials for various courses and departments.

School Uniforms

Official PE uniforms, lab gowns, and academic accessories.

School Supplies

High-quality notebooks, pens, folders, and essential stationery.

CvSU Merchandise

Branded T-shirts, mugs, caps, and university lanyards.
